Key Business Findings
Traffic — critical constraint
149/day avg
Average daily traffic is 149 — well below the ~211/day needed to hit ¥140M at current blended CVR (9%) and ATV (¥20,151). March averaged 178/day; April dropped to 120/day — a worsening trend heading into the season.
Significant April decline
−36% sales MoM
April sales fell 36% vs March despite CVR improving +1.7pp. The drivers: traffic dropped 33% and ATV fell 21%. Both traffic and basket size are moving in the wrong direction simultaneously.
CVR is improving
8.3% → 10.0%
Conversion improved month-on-month — the store is getting better at converting whoever walks in. In-store execution is working. The problem is purely upstream: not enough foot traffic reaching the door.
ATV erosion — watch closely
¥22K → ¥17.6K
ATV dropped ¥4,637 (−21%) month-on-month. Monitor basket composition — are customers trading down, or was March's strong ATV driven by a few high-value purchases? Both interpretations have different responses.
Apr 13 — data error
QTY −6,477
April 13 shows negative quantity (−6,477) and UPT −498 alongside ¥175K in sales. This is a system entry error or large return that must be investigated and corrected before any formal KPI reporting.
Mar 7 — event outlier
¥1.24M single day
March 7 generated ¥1.24M (17% of March revenue) with 471 traffic and 91 units. Almost certainly a group/corporate event. Stripping this, March's underlying daily run-rate is ~¥249K — still below the ¥383K target pace.

What needs to change to hit ¥140M?
Scenario A — Traffic only
149 → 211/day
Grow daily traffic by +62 visitors (+42%) while holding CVR and ATV flat. Requires sustained investment in awareness, SNS geo-tagging, Cat Street presence, and event programming.
Scenario B — CVR uplift only
9% → 12.8%
Hold traffic at 149/day and raise CVR from 9% to 12.8% (+3.8pp). Requires significant in-store improvements — clienteling, visual merchandising, staff training, or product mix restructuring.
Scenario C — ATV uplift only
¥20K → ¥28.3K
Hold traffic and CVR flat, grow ATV by 41%. Very difficult without a deliberate premium product push, bundle strategy, or significant change in the product assortment mix.
Scenario D — Balanced (recommended)
+20% each lever
Traffic 149→179, CVR 9%→10.8%, ATV ¥20K→¥24K. A balanced 20% uplift across all three levers generates ~¥144M — exceeding budget. The most resilient and realistic path to ¥140M.
